About

For bloggers in particular, having something visual in your blog posts is a rule. Finding images relevant to your posts can be difficult especially if covering multiple subjects. This tool creates nice images using others found by searching.

This tool was created by Dan Adams, a software engineer and technical consultant at Cantina Consulting.

Please don't steal images: check and respect the license of any images used and reference the image sources in your final material.
